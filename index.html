How to Play

  • GamePlay

    Each player reveals the top card of their stack simultaneously.

    The player with the highest card wins the round and collects the point

    In case of a tie (war), each tied player places three additional cards face down and then reveals the fourth card. The player with the highest fourth card wins all the cards on the table. If there's another tie, the process repeats until there's a clear winner.

  • Winning

    The game continues until the card count hits 0.
